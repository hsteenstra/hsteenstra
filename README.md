## 💫 About Me
I’m a high school developer, educator, and founder of **Code For ME**, an initiative focused on expanding access to computer science education across Maine.

After completing Harvard’s CS50, I began teaching coding, developing curriculum, and writing textbooks to make STEM education more accessible, practical, and impactful. My work is centered around not just learning to code—but using code to create real opportunities for others.

---

## 🚀 Code For ME
**Code For ME** is more than a project—it's a mission to bring computer science education to students who might not otherwise have access.

### 📘 Bring Coding to Your Classroom  
Our beginner-friendly textbook is designed for both students *and* teachers—no prior coding experience required.  
→ **Buy the textbook:** https://codeforme.org

### 🏕️ Join a Camp  
Hands-on coding camps where students build real projects in Python, web development, and more.  
→ **Sign up today:** https://codeforme.org

### 🎓 Learn. Build. Teach.  
We create structured curriculum, run programs, and empower students to become creators—not just consumers of technology.
