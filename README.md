## About

I am a high school developer, educator, and founder of Code For ME, an initiative focused on expanding access to computer science education across Maine.

After completing Harvard’s CS50, I began teaching coding, developing curriculum, and writing textbooks to make STEM education more accessible, practical, and scalable. My work centers on creating real opportunities for students to learn, build, and grow through technology.

---

## Code For ME

Code For ME is designed to make computer science education accessible to students and teachers regardless of prior experience.

### Textbook
A structured, beginner-friendly textbook designed for classrooms and independent learners.

Purchase here:  
https://payhip.com/CodeForME

### Programs
